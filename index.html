About Me

The Story
Behind the Sound

Hi, I'm Lawrence KushBurn — an independent songwriter and musician crafting music that blurs the lines between trapsoul, RnB, and loves storytelling.

I started making music in my bedroom with nothing but a dollar and a dream. Every track I release is a piece of my story, and every fan who buys directly from me helps me keep creating without compromise and I thank thoses for supporting.

This site is where you can own my music, go deeper with exclusive membership perks, and connect with a community of people who feel the same way about music creation freedom like we do.

Self-Produced Every lyric and note composed by myself
Fan-Funded No label. 100% independent
